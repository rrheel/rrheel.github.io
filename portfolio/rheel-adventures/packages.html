Our Adventure Packages

The itineraries below are subject to change, influenced by things such as season, weather, and the availability of special events. All itineraries include:

  • Flight
  • Ticket or fare from airport to hotel
  • Hotel (with complimentary breakfast)
  • Public transit transportation card
  • Tickets and fees for all activities

Itineraries are eligible for hotel and flight upgrades. Travelers may also pay extra to travel by taxi instead of subway or bus, as applicable.

For information on many of the attractions in and around Seoul, see our Seoulmate travel guide. We also recommend that you check the websites and guides listed on our Resources page!

*Russian Roulette Day:
Travelers can choose between three—sometimes four—events:

  • Seasonal Event: Attend a seasonal event like the Seoul Lantern Festival
  • K-pop Tour: Visit iconic spots associated with top K-pop groups like EXO, BLACKPINK, and BTS. Guaranteed visit to SMTown and Yookdang Sikdang, BTS's favorite restaurant
  • K-drama Tour: Sip an iced Americano at the café from Coffee Prince, blow out a candle at Jumunjin Breakwater to summon a dokkaebi like in Guardian, or have a meal at the restaurant featured in Weightlifting Fairy Kim Bok Joo!
  • DMZ tour: Visit the stretch of land that separates North Korea from South Korea

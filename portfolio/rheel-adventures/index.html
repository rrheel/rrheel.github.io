Featured Spot: Namsan Park

Google “Seoul,” and at least half of the images that pop up will include a tall white tower emerging from the green blanket of trees that cover Mount Namsan. This popular park has a lot to offer both Seoulites and visitors alike, including hiking trails, market days, performances, and the iconic N Seoul Tower. For an easy trip to the top, buy a ticket to ride the Namsan Cable Car, which has been featured in K-dramas like Boys over Flowers.

Break Free from the Predictable

Planning a trip to a new country can be exciting—but it can also be extremely stressful. Where should you stay? Where should you go? How should you get around? How should you handle the language barrier? It's enough to make you want to just book that beach vacation for the 10th consecutive year.

Enter Rheel Adventures. Our team has been to South Korea many times. We've used each of these visits to scour South Korea to find the best hotels, attractions, guided tours, restaurants, and more. There's no such thing as a perfect trip, but we'll get you pretty close.

Document Design

I chose Professional Writing as my major because it was something that I loved to do, and I could easily see myself going to school for four years to study the subject. I knew that I'd love the classes I'd have to take and and love learning more about all kinds of writing. But I couldn't have anticipated how much I enjoyed the design-related classes and taking plain text and turning it into something that's usable, readable, and draws the eye.

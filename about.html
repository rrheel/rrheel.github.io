About Me

Rehann Rheel is a technical writer and editor focused on improving documentation clarity and usability. She graduated from Chatham University in 2021 with a Master of Professional Writing. She’s worked at Bechtel Plant Machinery, Inc. since 2017, where she edits technical manuals used for large watercraft. While there, she’s also taken on special projects that include:

  • Acting as Lead for the system that maintains tech manual inventory
  • Redesigning that system to improve usability and efficiency
  • Creating and updating operating instructions
  • Training fellow tech writers

In her free time, she likes to travel—whether that be via a book, a video game, or a real-life plane. She currently resides in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania.
